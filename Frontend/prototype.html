Dolphin Cove Ocho Rios Jamaica

Ocho Ríos is a quintessential city that welcomes cruise travelers with white sandy beaches and swaying palm trees, where Dolphin Cove Ocho Ríos, a fantastic Dolphin Habitat and natural park framed by majestic lush tropical nature, is located.

How to Get to Dolphin Cove Ocho Rios?

Visiting Dolphin Cove Ocho Rios is very easy. However, it would be best if you considered important factors such as distance and traffic.

Dolphin Cove Ocho Rios is 4 km (2.5 mi) from the Cruise Port at Turtle Beach Road. If you are visiting Ocho Rios as a cruise traveler or staying at a lodge or resort in the northeast area, take a taxi or hop on a "route taxi" and prepare for your adventure.

If you are staying in the Montego Bay area, we recommend you visit the Dolphin Cove Montego Bay Dolphin Habitat , as Ocho Rios is 97 km (60 mi) away.
